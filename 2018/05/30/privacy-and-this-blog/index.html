Privacy and This Blog

This blog does not have Google Analytics. It doesn’t have any tracking at all. The only thing that might do some tracking are the Disqus conversations at the bottom. I don’t know who reads my blog, I don’t know where my readers are from, or how they came to be on my site. And I did this intentionally. Reflecting on privacy and how it’s generally being eroded made me realize how I hate being tracked across websites. Working in big data makes me realize how easy it is to collect and analyze user’s behaviour.
