OPDS

Overview

OPDS is a standard for distributing media, specifically ebooks. books has OPDS support and offers the following catalogs for navigation and acquisition:

  • Alphabetic, Title: books by title
  • Random Books: random selection of books
  • Recently Added Books: books by added date
  • Recently Published Books: orders books by publication date
  • The view for an individual book allows downloading books in all the registerd formats

Usage

OPDS support is automatically enabled. The OPDS root feed is available under <server:port>/opds/. The trailing slash is required.
