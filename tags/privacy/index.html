Privacy and This Blog

30 May 2018 , tagged: privacy, blog

This blog does not have Google Analytics. It doesn’t have any tracking at all. The only thing that might do some tracking are the Disqus conversations at the bottom. I don’t know who reads my blog, I don’t know where my readers are from, or how they came to be on my site. And I did this intentionally. Reflecting on privacy and how it’s generally being eroded made me realize how I hate being tracked across websites.
