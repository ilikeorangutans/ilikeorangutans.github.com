[DRAFT] Inheritance and Composition

17 Jun 2018 , tagged: OOP, Software Development, Object Oriented Design, Liskov Substitution Principle

I was lucky enough to attend an Object Oriented Programming workshop by Sandi Metz this month. The workshop itself was fantastic and showed me a beautiful side of OOP that I haven’t seen before. It also spurred some great discussions with coworkers and one of the topics we talked about is inheritance over composition. Based on my experience I generally favour composition over inheritance, but that has mostly to do with poor inheritance hierarchies I’ve had the pleasure of working with.
